About

We live in a digital era where time stops for none. Although we have progressed manifold in terms of technology, digitalization, wisdom and research there is one aspect of our lifestyles which remains untouched and unaltered. Be it any age group, people often face issues in their lifestyles. These issues give birth to unproductivity, forlorness, low self esteem, dissatisfaction. Such mental problems should not be sidelined.Maintaining a positive mental health and treating any mental health conditions is crucial to stabilizing constructive behaviors, emotions, and thoughts. Focusing on mental health will increase productivity, enhance self image and improve relationships.
This project aims to be your one stop destination whenever you feel burdened by stress. You may access a calmer state of mind with just a click the next time you experience a weight of stress, just like carrying a therapist in your pocket!
